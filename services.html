Our Services

Supporting our community through all stages of life

Funeral Services (Janazah)

We provide comprehensive funeral services for the Muslim community, including:

  • Guidance and support during bereavement
  • Ghusl (ritual washing) facilities
  • Kafan (shrouding) services
  • Janazah prayer at the mosque
  • Liaison with funeral directors

For urgent assistance, please contact us immediately.

Contact for Janazah Services

Marriage Services (Nikah)

We offer Islamic marriage ceremonies conducted according to the Sunni tradition:

  • Pre-marital guidance and counselling
  • Nikah ceremony at the mosque
  • Islamic marriage certificate
  • Guidance on Islamic marital rights and responsibilities

Please contact us in advance to arrange a Nikah ceremony.

Counselling

We provide confidential counselling and support services:

  • Marriage and family counselling
  • Spiritual guidance
  • Support during difficult times
  • Referrals to specialist services when needed

All counselling is strictly confidential.
