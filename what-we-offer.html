What We Offer

Services, programs, and gatherings for our community

Services

Supporting our community through all stages of life

Funeral Services (Janazah)

We provide comprehensive funeral services for the Muslim community, including:

  • Guidance and support during bereavement
  • Ghusl (ritual washing) facilities
  • Kafan (shrouding) services
  • Janazah prayer at the mosque
  • Transportation to cemetery included
  • Liaison with funeral directors

For urgent assistance, please contact us immediately.

Contact for Janazah Services

Marriage Services (Nikah)

We offer Islamic marriage ceremonies conducted according to the Sunni tradition:

  • Pre-marital guidance and counselling
  • Nikah ceremony at the mosque
  • Islamic marriage certificate
  • Guidance on Islamic marital rights and responsibilities

Please contact us in advance to arrange a Nikah ceremony.

Enquire About Nikah Services

Counselling

We provide confidential counselling and support services:

  • Marriage and family counselling
  • Spiritual guidance
  • Support during difficult times
  • Referrals to specialist services when needed

All counselling is strictly confidential.

Gatherings

Coming together to commemorate and celebrate

Gyarwee Shareef

Monthly (usually second Sunday)

Monthly gatherings commemorating Shaykh ʿAbd al-Qādir al-Jīlānī (may Allah be pleased with him), one of the most revered saints in Islamic history. These gatherings include Quran recitation, Hamd, Naat, Manqabats, and the sharing of food.

Dates may vary. Check our social media for announcements.

Sponsor a Gyarwee and dedicate the reward to your departed loved ones.

Learn about Gyarwee Contact Us to Sponsor

Milad-un-Nabi (Mawlid)

Main celebration in Rabi' al-Awwal

We celebrate the birth of Prophet Muhammad (peace, blessings and salutations be upon him) with gatherings that include Quran recitation, Salawat, Na'at and Qasida, and talks on the Prophet's life and teachings.

Our main celebration is held during the blessed month of Rabi' al-Awwal, with smaller Mehfil-e-Milad gatherings throughout the year.
